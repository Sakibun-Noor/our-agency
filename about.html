/ 01 — Why this exists

We were tired of handoffs.

Most agencies hand you a deck and a Slack channel, then disappear into Notion until the invoice arrives. We hated that as clients, and we hated it as operators.

Digital Harbor is what happens when strategy, design, engineering, and growth sit on one Linear board, in one weekly cadence, with one bill at the end of the quarter.

We turn down more work than we take. That's the only way the math works.
